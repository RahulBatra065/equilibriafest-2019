About Us

Equilibria’20, SSE’s annual cultural festival is back with more embellishment and enthusiasm than ever before! Equilibria stands with a wide plethora of events, ranging from Academics to Gaming and a huge platform for students to congregate and be part of SSE. Equilibria perceives a continuous growth in passion and participation from students all across the country, every year. And this year, EQ gets bigger and better! With an endless aim of encouraging youth, EQ conducts competitions neck to neck.
